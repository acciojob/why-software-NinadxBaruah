Why I want to become a developer?

I have always been fascinated by the way technology can be used to solve problems and make people's lives easier. As a software developer, I would have the skills and knowledge to create software that meets the needs of users and helps them achieve their goals. I am also drawn to the constantly evolving nature of the field, as there is always something new to learn and discover. Overall, becoming a software developer would allow me to combine my passions for technology and problem-solving, and make a positive impact in the world through my work.
